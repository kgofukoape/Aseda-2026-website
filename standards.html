What SHEQ Means at Aseda

Four Pillars.
One Non-Negotiable
Standard.

Aseda's SHEQ framework is built on the understanding that safe, healthy, environmentally responsible, and high-quality delivery are not separate workstreams — they are one integrated standard that governs every decision we make on and off site.

Our SHEQ policy is reviewed annually, embedded in every contract, and enforced with the same rigour as our technical and commercial obligations. Every Aseda employee, subcontractor, and site visitor is accountable to it.

S
Safety
Protection of all people on and around our sites — employees, subcontractors, communities, and visitors. Zero harm is our baseline commitment, not a stretch target.
H
Health
Proactive management of occupational health risks — dust, noise, vibration, and chemical exposure — ensuring long-term wellbeing for everyone in the Aseda community.
E
Environment
Responsible stewardship of the natural environment through rigorous impact assessments, waste management protocols, and continuous monitoring throughout the project lifecycle.
Q
Quality
Unbendable Quality on every deliverable — governed by our ISO 9001:2015 certified QMS, which documents, measures, and continuously improves every process we execute.
S
Safety

Every Worker.
Every Site.
Every Day.

Our safety programme is built on the principle that all injuries and incidents are preventable. We do not accept risk as an inevitable feature of construction — we systematically identify, assess, and eliminate it before work begins.

Site Safety Plans
Comprehensive Safety Plans developed for every project before mobilisation — covering hazard identification, risk assessment, and emergency response procedures.
OHS Act Compliant
Safety Officer Deployment
Dedicated SHEQ Officers deployed on all active construction sites — conducting daily inspections, toolbox talks, and compliance monitoring.
Mandatory on all sites
Incident Reporting & Investigation
All incidents and near-misses are reported, investigated, and closed out with corrective actions — creating a continuous learning loop that improves our safety performance over time.
Zero tolerance for under-reporting
Emergency Response Readiness
Site-specific Emergency Response Plans in place on all projects, with regular drills and clearly communicated evacuation and first-aid procedures for all personnel.
Tested quarterly
H
Health

Protecting People
Beyond the
Visible Risks.

Construction carries occupational health risks that often manifest years after exposure. Our health programme addresses both immediate hazards and long-term risks — ensuring that working at Aseda never compromises the health of our people.

Occupational Health Risk Assessment
Systematic identification and management of occupational exposure to dust, noise, vibration, UV radiation, and hazardous substances on all active projects.
OHSAS Standards
PPE Standards & Enforcement
Mandatory Personal Protective Equipment protocols enforced across all sites — from basic hi-vis and hard hats to specialised respiratory and hearing protection where required.
Non-negotiable on all sites
Wellness & Fatigue Management
Structured working hour controls and fatigue management protocols — particularly critical on long-distance road and rural infrastructure projects where worker fatigue poses heightened risk.
COIDA registered — 9900004020298
Employee Assistance Programme
Access to confidential support resources for physical and mental wellbeing — recognising that health encompasses the whole person, not just physical safety on site.
Available to all staff
E
Environment

We Build on the
Land. We are
Responsible to It.

Infrastructure development always interacts with the natural environment. At Aseda, we take this responsibility seriously — embedding environmental stewardship into our project planning, execution, and close-out processes as a matter of course.

Environmental Impact Assessment
Comprehensive EIAs conducted and adhered to for all applicable projects — ensuring legal compliance and proactive management of ecological impact.
NEMA Compliant
Waste Management & Rehabilitation
Structured waste segregation, disposal, and site rehabilitation protocols ensuring that the land we work on is restored to its pre-construction condition on project completion.
NEMA Waste Act Aligned
Water & Stormwater Management
Site-specific water management plans controlling runoff, sedimentation, and contamination risk — particularly critical on water infrastructure and road projects near waterways.
DWS Guidelines Compliant
Carbon & Energy Monitoring
Ongoing monitoring of energy consumption and carbon footprint across operations — supporting our transition to more sustainable delivery practices through Aseda Energy.
Reported annually

Our Policy Commitments

What We
Commit To.
In Writing.

Our SHEQ policy is signed by executive leadership and communicated to every employee, subcontractor, and stakeholder who works with Aseda. These are not aspirations — they are binding commitments.

Safety Commitment
"We are committed to providing a working environment in which all injuries and occupational diseases are preventable. We will not compromise the safety and health of any person for the sake of programme or profit."
Environmental Commitment
"We will identify, assess, and manage all environmental impacts associated with our activities — leaving every site in a condition equal to or better than we found it."
Quality Commitment
"We will deliver Unbendable Quality on every project — meeting client specifications, statutory requirements, and our own internal standards without exception or compromise."
Site Standards

What We Require
On Every Site

01
Pre-Commencement Safety File

Every project requires a complete Safety File before mobilisation — including the Safety Plan, risk assessments, method statements, and contractor competency records.

OHS Act Section 7 · Construction Regulations
02
Daily Toolbox Talks

Mandatory daily safety briefings conducted by the site Safety Officer — covering the day's activities, associated hazards, and required controls. Attendance is recorded for every worker.

Site mandatory — all personnel
03
Subcontractor SHEQ Compliance

All subcontractors and suppliers appointed on Aseda projects are required to demonstrate SHEQ compliance as a condition of appointment — our standards extend through the full supply chain.

Pre-qualification mandatory
04
PPE Provision & Enforcement

Appropriate PPE is provided to all workers — hard hats, hi-vis, safety boots, gloves, eye and hearing protection as required by the specific risk profile of each site activity.

SANS 1397 & SANS 434 Compliant
05
Site Induction Programme

All workers — including visitors — receive a site-specific safety induction before being permitted on site. No exceptions. Induction records are retained throughout the project lifecycle.

Before access — no exceptions
06
Monthly SHEQ Audits

Independent monthly SHEQ audits conducted on all active sites — findings are reported to project management and executive leadership, with corrective actions tracked to closure.
