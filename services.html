02
Water & Sanitation Infrastructure

Water is a
Human Right.
We Engineer It.

Recognising water as a primary lever for human dignity, we deliver engineering solutions that protect and provide this critical resource.

Our water and sanitation practice addresses the full spectrum of infrastructure needs — from bulk supply schemes serving entire districts to wastewater rehabilitation critical to environmental protection. We bring rigorous engineering to some of South Africa's most pressing infrastructure backlogs.

View Water & Sanitation Projects →
Bulk Water Supply
Planning and design of reservoirs, pump stations, rising mains, and bulk pipelines for district-scale water provision programmes.
Wastewater Treatment
Engineering of modern treatment plants, sludge lagoon rehabilitation, and WWTP upgrades aligned to environmental sustainability standards.
Hydrological Modelling
Precision analysis for stormwater management, flood-line determination, and catchment-level water resource assessment.
Water Conservation
Non-revenue water reduction programmes, infrastructure retrofitting, and demand management for metropolitan municipalities.
Sewer & Sanitation Networks
Design, rehabilitation, and capacity upgrades of sewer reticulation networks to prevent environmental contamination and accommodate urban growth.
Track Record Highlight
R350M Magalies WWTP Upgrade · R190M Chris Hani District Municipality Water Supply · R85M Tsakane "War on Leaks" · R52M Steynsrus Water Supply Scheme
